Working at Game Day Grill

small team, big impact

Game Day Grill fosters an exciting and animated environment. As it is a sports grill, we promote teamwork, enthusiasm, and a passion for serving the Game Day Grill community. Working at Game Day Grill is not just being an employee, it is a team. Our employees are encouraged to always work to create a fun, sport-loving grill. The work atmosphere is fast-paced but fun, as well as a collaborative effort to further develop the Game Day Grill experience. Our work culture celebrates the love shared over sports and food.


Benefits for Employees

  • Employee Meals: Food on us! At the end of each shift, you have the opportunity to take home food
  • Flexible Schedules: YOU choose when you would like to work, we work with you!
  • Paid Time Off (PTO): Full-time employees will receive paid time off for vacation, and sick leave
  • Competitive Bonuses: We award an OUTSTANDING employee each month- gifting them a bonus
  • Meal Prep & Cook Trainings: Trainings to improve your confidence in the kitchen!

Job Positions

Chef: Responsible for timely order creation and kitchen staff supervision

Line Cook: Prepares and cooks menu items according to standards

Prep Cook: Assists with food preparation task

Host/Hostess: Greets and seats guests, manages reservations

Waiter/Waitress: Takes orders, serves food, beverages, and creates a pleasant dining experience

Bartender: Prepares and serves beverages, with a speciality in mocktails

Busser: Clears and resets tables, refills water glasses, and assists servers

Dishwasher: Responsible for washing dishes, utensils, and kitchen equipment
