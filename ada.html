ADA Statement

Game Day Grill does not discriminate on the basis of disability in our services. We are dedicated to create a more accessible Game Day Grill experience, for everyone. We understand that accesibility is different for everyone, and we continue to work to improve the experience at Game Day Grill for everyone.

Website Accesibility

We strive to add onto the accessibility of our webiste. If there are any dificulties in accessing our website, please contact us at ada@gamedaygrill.com or XXX-XXX-XXXX.

Physical Accesibility

Our Game Day Grill location is designed create a fun exeperience for everyone. We have implemented ramps, accessible bathrooms, and big doorways.

Accomodation Support

Please do not hesitate to reach out to our staff if you desire any additional assistance. Our staff is trained to provide accomodations and support for individuals with disabilities, whether its sorting seating arrangements, meeting specific orders, or providing support. The Game Day Grill Team is commited to make your exeperience our top priority.
